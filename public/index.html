About Me

I am pursuing a double major in Applied Statistics and Informatics/Data Analytics and Visualization at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign (UIUC). My research focus is how to leverage Machine Learning in Finance and International Development. I have expertise in Spatial Econometrics, Financial Econometrics, Supervised and Unsupervised learning, Reinforcement learning and Time Series Analysis with application in portfolio selection, option pricing and credit card fraud detection. I can implement these models using Python or R. Before coming to UIUC, I got a master’s degree in Applied Economics from the University of Oklahoma as a Fulbright scholar, with hands-on experience in climate change modelling and optimal math programming in GAMS. I speak English, French, Adja, Mina and Fon.
